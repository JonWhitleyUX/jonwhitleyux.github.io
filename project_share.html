I wanted to try my hand at designing an app that would handle buying and selling stocks, and at the same time I didn't just want it to be another investing app. So the point of this app was never to be an "investing app", but rather to be something akin to a PayPal or a Venmo, but instead of directly handling money, you would instead be directly trading stocks.

So I started, as I like to do, by writing down a list of the desired and necessary functionalities, and once those were assembled I started to get an idea of what the app would like, so I dove straight into a sketch. I did a rough sketch of the wireframe; you can see both my list of features and my sketches below.

Once this stage was complete I proceeded on to the stage of doing a simplified low-grade computer wire-frame. I changed some elements of the design from left to right so that the elements may be more easily reached by right-handed users. You can see those renders below:
